# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, technical collaboration.

Do:

- keep discussion focused on the change, evidence, and project boundary
- explain risks and tradeoffs clearly
- respect maintainers' decisions about scope, timing, and repository ownership

Do not:

- harass, threaten, or target people
- publish private information
- use issues or reviews for unrelated promotion or personal attacks

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove contributions that break these
rules. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocked participation.
